Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ResumeRadar

For Job Seekers

How do I create a profile?

Click the "Register" button on the homepage, select "Job Seeker" as your role, fill in your details, and complete your profile. You'll be ready to start applying for jobs in minutes!

What file formats do you accept for resumes?

We accept PDF, DOCX, and TXT files. Maximum file size is 16MB. For best results, we recommend using PDF format as it preserves formatting and is most compatible with our parser.

How does the matching work?

Our platform extracts skills, experience, and education from your resume using text extraction (PyPDF2 for PDFs, python-docx for DOCX files). It then compares this information against job descriptions using TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity to calculate a match score.

Can I apply to multiple jobs?

Absolutely! You can apply to as many jobs as you want. We recommend focusing on positions where your match score is 60% or higher for the best chances.

How do I track my applications?

You can track all your applications from your dashboard. You'll see real-time status updates including "Pending", "Shortlisted", and "Hired". You'll also receive notifications when your application status changes.

Is it free to use for job seekers?

Yes! Creating a profile, uploading your resume, and applying to jobs is completely free for job seekers.

Can I save jobs to apply later?

Yes! You can save jobs you're interested in by clicking the bookmark icon. Saved jobs can be accessed from your dashboard under "Saved Jobs".

How do I know if my application was received?

After applying, the application status will show as "Pending" in your dashboard. You'll be notified when the recruiter reviews your application.

What happens after I'm shortlisted?

When shortlisted, you'll receive a notification. The recruiter may contact you for an interview or additional information. Check your dashboard for next steps.

How do I improve my match score?

Complete your profile with detailed skills, experience, and education. Use relevant keywords from job descriptions and keep your resume updated with your latest achievements.

What should I include in my resume?

Include your work experience, skills, education, certifications, and achievements. Use bullet points to highlight accomplishments and quantify results where possible.

Can I upload multiple resumes?

You can upload one primary resume, but you can update it anytime. For different job types, you can tailor your resume before applying.

For Recruiters

How do I post a job?

After logging in as a recruiter, click "Post Job" from your dashboard. Fill in the job title, description, requirements, location, salary range, and other details. Your job will be published immediately and visible to job seekers.

How do I review candidates?

Visit your job listing and click "View Candidates". You'll see all applicants ranked by their match score. You can filter by score, status, and more. Click on any candidate to view their full profile and resume.

Can I shortlist candidates?

Yes! You can shortlist candidates directly from the candidates list using the "Shortlist" button. Shortlisted candidates will receive a notification and can be easily filtered in your dashboard.

How accurate is the matching?

The matching uses TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity — the same techniques used in search engines. It ranks candidates based on keyword relevance and skill matching. Match scores should be used as a guide, not as the sole decision factor.

Can I export candidate data?

Yes! You can export candidate lists to CSV format. This includes candidate names, scores, contact information, and application status for offline analysis.

How do I reject candidates professionally?

Use the "Reject" button on a candidate's profile. The candidate will receive a polite rejection notification with constructive feedback to help them improve.

What happens when a job is filled?

You can mark the job as "Hired" to close it. The job will no longer accept new applications, and candidates will see it as filled. Notifications will be sent to all applicants.

Can I communicate with candidates directly?

Yes, you can view candidate contact information (email, phone) from their profile. All communications are tracked and stored for reference.

How do I search for specific candidates?

Use the search bar to find candidates by name, email, phone, or skills. You can also filter by match score, status, location, and experience level.

Is there a limit on job postings?

Recruiters can create job postings without a hard limit. Jobs can be closed when positions are filled.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires in 1 hour for security.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt, and we never store sensitive information in plain text. The application is hosted on Render's secure cloud platform.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email address in profile settings. The new email will be used for all future communications and login attempts.

What happens if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive a password reset email with instructions to create a new password.

Can I have multiple accounts?

Users are limited to one account per email address. If you need both recruiter and job seeker access, you'll need to create separate accounts with different email addresses.
