Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HireSmart

For Job Seekers

How do I create a profile?

Click the "Register" button on the homepage, select "Job Seeker" as your role, fill in your details, and complete your profile. You'll be ready to start applying for jobs in minutes!

What file formats do you accept for resumes?

We accept PDF, DOCX, and TXT files. Maximum file size is 10MB. For best results, we recommend using PDF format as it preserves formatting and is most compatible with our AI parser.

How does the AI matching work?

Our AI analyzes your resume to extract skills, experience, education, and other qualifications. It then compares this information against job descriptions to calculate a match score. The AI understands synonyms (e.g., "5 years" = "five years") and related skills (e.g., Python and Django are related).

Can I apply to multiple jobs?

Absolutely! You can apply to as many jobs as you want. We recommend focusing on positions where your match score is 60% or higher for the best chances.

How do I track my applications?

You can track all your applications from your dashboard. You'll see real-time status updates including "Pending", "Shortlisted", "Interview", and "Hired". You'll also receive email notifications when your application status changes.

Is it free to use for job seekers?

Yes! Creating a profile, uploading your resume, and applying to jobs is completely free for job seekers. You can apply to unlimited jobs without any cost.

How often should I update my resume?

We recommend updating your resume whenever you gain new skills, complete a project, or change jobs. Regular updates help our AI provide better job matches.

Can I save jobs to apply later?

Yes! You can save jobs you're interested in by clicking the bookmark icon. Saved jobs can be accessed from your dashboard under "Saved Jobs".

How do I know if my application was received?

After applying, you'll receive a confirmation email and the application status will show as "Pending" in your dashboard. You'll be notified when the recruiter reviews your application.

What happens after I'm shortlisted?

When shortlisted, you'll receive an email notification. The recruiter may contact you for an interview or additional information. Check your dashboard for next steps.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from profile settings. This action is permanent and will remove all your data from our system.

How do I improve my match score?

Complete your profile with detailed skills, experience, and education. Use relevant keywords from job descriptions and keep your resume updated with your latest achievements.

What should I include in my resume?

Include your work experience, skills, education, certifications, and achievements. Use bullet points to highlight accomplishments and quantify results where possible.

Can I upload multiple resumes?

You can upload one primary resume, but you can update it anytime. For different job types, you can tailor your resume before applying.

For Recruiters

How do I post a job?

After logging in as a recruiter, click "Post Job" from your dashboard. Fill in the job title, description, requirements, location, salary range, and other details. Your job will be published immediately and visible to job seekers.

How do I review candidates?

Visit your job listing and click "View Candidates". You'll see all applicants ranked by their AI match score. You can filter by score, status, skills, and more. Click on any candidate to view their full profile and resume.

Can I shortlist candidates?

Yes! You can shortlist candidates directly from the candidates list. Shortlisted candidates will receive an email notification and can be easily filtered in your dashboard for quick access.

How accurate is the AI matching?

Our AI achieves 95% accuracy in matching candidates to jobs. It uses multiple algorithms including keyword matching, semantic similarity, skill relationship mapping, and experience calculation to ensure the most relevant candidates rise to the top.

Is there a limit on job postings?

Basic accounts can post up to 10 active jobs. Premium accounts offer unlimited job postings, featured listings, and advanced analytics. Contact our sales team for enterprise plans.

Can I export candidate data?

Yes! You can export candidate lists to Excel format. This includes candidate names, scores, contact information, and application status for offline analysis.

How do I reject candidates professionally?

Use the "Reject" button on a candidate's profile. The candidate will receive a polite rejection email with constructive feedback to help them improve.

Can I edit a posted job?

Yes, you can edit your job postings at any time. Changes are immediately reflected for candidates viewing the job.

What happens when a job is filled?

You can mark the job as "Hired" to close it. The job will no longer accept new applications, and candidates will see it as filled.

Can I communicate with candidates directly?

Yes, you can send messages to candidates through the platform. All communications are tracked and stored for reference.

How do I search for specific candidates?

Use the search bar to find candidates by name, email, phone, or skills. You can also filter by match score, status, location, and experience level.

Can I create candidate shortlists?

Yes! You can create custom shortlists for different positions. Shortlisted candidates can be easily accessed from your dashboard.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires in 1 hour for security.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt, and we never store sensitive information in plain text. We're GDPR compliant and regularly audited for security.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email address in profile settings. You'll need to verify the new email address before it becomes active.

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

2FA is available for premium accounts. Go to Security Settings in your profile to enable it. You'll need an authenticator app like Google Authenticator.

What happens if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive a password reset email with instructions to create a new password.

Can I have multiple accounts?

Users are limited to one account per email address. If you need both recruiter and job seeker access, contact support for assistance.

How do I report suspicious activity?

Contact our support team immediately at security@hiresmart.com or use the "Report" button on any suspicious profile or job posting.

How long is my data retained?

We retain your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, data is removed within 30 days, except where required for legal compliance.

What's included in premium plans?

Do you offer enterprise plans?

Technical Support

We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome.

How do I report a bug?
