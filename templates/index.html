Why Choose Our Platform

Recruitment doesn't have to be a guessing game. We built a platform that brings structure, transparency and speed to a process that has been broken for too long.

Intelligent Skill Matching

Every resume is compared against the job description using TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity — the same techniques used in enterprise search engines. Candidates are ranked not just by keywords but by the depth and relevance of their skill profile.

TF-IDF Matching Real-time

Transparent Candidate Scoring

Every applicant receives a detailed breakdown across four dimensions — technical skills, soft skills, years of experience and education level. No black box. No guesswork. Recruiters see exactly why a candidate scored high or low.

4 Metrics Visual Reports

Automated Resume Intelligence

Upload a PDF or DOCX file and the platform handles the rest. Skills, experience, education and certifications are automatically extracted and structured — eliminating hours of manual reading for every open position.

Auto-extract All Formats

From Application to Shortlist in Seconds

The moment a candidate applies their resume is scored against the job description automatically. Recruiters open their dashboard to a ranked list — not an inbox full of attachments. The highest scoring candidates rise to the top instantly.

Instant Ranking Instant Results

How It Works

1

Create Your Account

Sign up in under two minutes as a recruiter or job seeker. No credit card. No complicated setup. Just choose your role and you are ready to go.

✓ Free registration ✓ Recruiter and job seeker roles ✓ Google sign-in supported
2

Upload Your Resume or Post a Job

Job seekers upload their resume in PDF or DOCX format. Recruiters post detailed job descriptions with required skills and experience. The platform handles everything else automatically.

✓ PDF/DOCX supported ✓ Automatic text extraction ✓ Skills parsed instantly
3

Get Your Match Score

The moment a resume enters the system it is scored against the job description using cosine similarity and TF-IDF analysis. Every candidate receives a breakdown across technical skills, soft skills, experience and education.

✓ Scored across four dimensions ✓ Transparent ranking ✓ Instant results
4

Make Your Decision

Recruiters shortlist, reject or hire candidates directly from their dashboard. Every status change triggers an automatic notification to the candidate. Job seekers always know exactly where they stand.

✓ One click shortlisting ✓ Automatic candidate notifications ✓ Full application history
