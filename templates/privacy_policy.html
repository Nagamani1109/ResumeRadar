Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and account preferences
  • Profile Information: Resume, work experience, education, skills, and professional details you choose to upload
  • Job Application Data: Jobs you apply for, application status, and related communications
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our platform, including pages viewed and features used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services:

  • Match candidates with relevant job opportunities
  • Help recruiters find qualified candidates
  • Send application updates and important notifications
  • Improve our matching algorithms and platform features
  • Provide support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure platform security

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Employers: When you apply to a job, your profile and resume are shared with the employer
  • With Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in operating our platform (hosting, database, email delivery)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Secure hosting on Render cloud platform
  • Access controls and authentication protocols

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at naga2005mani@gmail.com

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies required for platform functionality. These include:

  • Session Cookies: Required to keep you logged in
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings.
